    Introducing Jiyuu Linux

    Jiyuu: The Japanese concept of freedom, often translated to “as you like.” We believe that users, regardless of their skill level, should be able to make their own choices about the software they run. Unlike other entry-level distributions, the flexibility afforded to us by the Gentoo base means that users wont outgrow their systems; every component is modular, every aspect customizable. Jiyuu Linux is developed by every-day Linux users with the intention of creating the first entry-level distribution that will focus on teaching the user how to use Linux through hands-on Linux use.

    Yum & PackageKit
    Jiyuu Linux uses Yum for package management with the PackageKit frontend, making installing and upgrading your packages simple and easy.
